Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your username and password (stored securely as a hash).
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Basic usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used only to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To display your expenses and summaries back to you.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app reliability.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces for the interface — this involves a request to Google's servers when you load a page, but no personal data is transmitted. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.
